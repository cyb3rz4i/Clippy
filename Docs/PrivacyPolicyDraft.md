# Clippy Privacy Policy Draft

Clippy stores clipboard history locally on your Mac. Clipboard entries may include text, links, and images you copy after enabling clipboard history.

Clippy does not create accounts, sync data to cloud services, sell data, share data with third parties, use analytics, use advertising SDKs, or require network access.

Clipboard history is stored locally on your Mac. You can delete individual items, clear all history, pause capture, disable capture, or exclude specific apps from capture.

If auto-paste is enabled and you grant macOS Accessibility permission, Clippy can paste the selected clipboard item into the app you were using. Without that permission, selecting an item copies it to the pasteboard for manual paste.

Launch at login is optional and can be changed in Settings.
